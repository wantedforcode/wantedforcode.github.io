Home Careers

Senior Engineers Only

We don't hire in bulk. We work with a small, focused team of senior engineers who take full ownership of their work. If you want to be part of a team that ships real software for real clients — without middlemen or bloated process — we want to hear from you.

We don't post open roles — we find the right people. If that's you, reach out.

What We Look For

Technical Depth

5+ years of real production experience. You've owned systems end-to-end and made architecture decisions you'd defend today.

Clear Communication

You can explain complex technical decisions to a non-technical stakeholder without talking down to them.

Ownership Mindset

You care about what ships, not just what you write. Quality, reliability, and documentation are non-negotiable.

Think You're the Right Fit?

Send us a short note introducing yourself and what you've built. No formal CV required — tell us who you are and what you care about.

Send a Note

Senior engineers working directly with your team. No account managers, no layers in between.
