Home Services

Senior Engineering. Three Ways.

Three services, all delivered by senior engineers with direct client access. No junior handoffs, no bloated teams.

Custom Software Development

Custom software development produces web applications, APIs, and backend systems built to your specification. Clean architecture, tested code, engineers who understand your domain.

Technical Leadership

Fractional technical leadership gives you system design, stack decisions, architecture reviews, and engineering team direction — without the full-time hire. Useful when you've raised a round and need to restructure the tech team, when legacy debt is blocking delivery, or when your internal team needs a senior reference without adding management layers.

Product Engineering

Product engineering covers the full software lifecycle — from MVP definition through to reliable production deployment. Planning, building, deploying, and iterating until the system holds under real load.
