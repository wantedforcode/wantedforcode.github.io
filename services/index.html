What We Build

Home Services

Senior Engineering. Six Disciplines.

Six disciplines, all delivered by senior engineers with direct client access. No junior handoffs, no bloated teams.

Custom Software Development

Custom software development produces web applications, APIs, and backend systems built to your specification. Clean architecture, tested code, engineers who understand your domain.

Technical Leadership

Fractional technical leadership gives you system design, stack decisions, architecture reviews, and engineering team direction — without the full-time hire. Useful when you've raised a round and need to restructure the tech team, when legacy debt is blocking delivery, or when your internal team needs a senior reference without adding management layers.

Product Engineering

Product engineering covers the full software lifecycle — from MVP definition through to reliable production deployment. Planning, building, deploying, and iterating until the system holds under real load.

Legacy Modernisation

Legacy modernisation audits aging codebases to surface EOL risks, exposed data, and unmapped APIs. The output is a concrete migration path to modern infrastructure, not a generic report.

Systems Integration

Systems integration connects existing software to ERPs, APIs, and IoT infrastructure through clean integration layers. Built to hold under real production load, not just demo conditions.

Applied AI Engineering

Applied AI engineering means building the system, not buying the platform. We automate business processes, integrate LLMs, build MCP servers and clients, design agentic architectures, and implement RAG pipelines — directly into your production stack. Platform-agnostic: OpenAI, Anthropic, or open models, depending on what the problem requires.
